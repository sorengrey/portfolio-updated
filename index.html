Heather Smith

About Me

Heather Smith is a developer currently enrolled in Georgia Tech's Full Stack Flex Bootcamp. Her skills include:

  • JavaScript/jQuery
  • HTML/CSS (including Bootstrap)
  • Postman
  • Express.js
  • Node.js
  • MySQL
  • Handlebars

She also has a strong understanding of design, color theory, and composition.

Before the pandemic, Heather was working as an office administrator for a small label printing company. Since experts advised everyone to stay home, she did just that and started self-guided studies with Codecademy. After studying through the summer and fall, she had built a foundation for coding. It was time to move forward. So, she enrolled in a bootcamp in hopes of starting a new career in summer 2021.

When she isn't coding, Heather likes gardening, hiking, cooking, thrift shopping, and video games. She also loves to fossil hunt, and is a member of the Georgia Mineral Society. Currently, she lives in Sandy Springs, Georgia with her husband and two cats.
